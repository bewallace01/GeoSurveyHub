GeoSurveyHub

What we are

GeoSurveyHub is a free site for people who work in surveying and GIS — party chiefs, crew members, drone pilots, and anyone who lives in maps and data. The idea is simple: put what you need to know about the industry in one place so you spend less time researching and more time deciding—compare gear, learn the tech, and follow new developments as they land.

How we write

Pages aim to be accurate and easy to read, with tradeoffs spelled out when “best” depends on terrain, budget, and deliverables. We do not provide legal, financial, or professional surveying services—always verify requirements with your jurisdiction and clients.

We don’t sell equipment

We do not sell equipment or originate loans. If you’re looking to buy gear and need funding, we connect you with Axiant Partners — they help businesses find loans and leases for equipment. Credit and terms are handled by Axiant and their lending partners, not by GeoSurveyHub.

About this website

GeoSurveyHub is designed and built by Wallace Web Workers, a Charlotte-based web studio. If you are evaluating a professional site for your own firm, that page explains how we approach performance, content, and edge infrastructure.
