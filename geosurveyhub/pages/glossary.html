Definitions

Quick terms

DTM / DSM
Digital terrain model (bare earth) vs digital surface model (includes tops of objects).
GSD
Ground sample distance—pixel size on the ground for imagery or orthos.
RMSE
Root mean square error; common statistic for checking control accuracy.
Multipath
GNSS signal reflections that degrade position—common near buildings and metal.
PPK / RTK
Post-processed vs real-time kinematic corrections—see PPK vs RTK.
Point cloud
Large set of XYZ points with optional attributes—from LiDAR or dense matching.
