Point Cloud Processing

Field capture is half the story. Processing turns millions of points into surfaces, vectors, and client-ready files—ground separation, noise stripping, and format conversion are where projects succeed or slip.

Typical workflow

1. Ingest & register — Combine flights or scans; verify control.

2. Clean — Remove outliers and multipath noise.

3. Classify — Ground vs non-ground; refine with breaklines where needed.

4. Deliver — DTM/DSM, contours, classified LAS/LAZ, CAD, or GIS layers.

AI-assisted steps

See AI & machine learning for how modern tools assist classification—always budget QA on edges (steep terrain, water, thin objects).

Hardware note

Large projects stress CPUs/GPUs and storage—workstations & field PCs matter as much as the sensor.
